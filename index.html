How to play

  • The number key row (from ` to =) plays bass notes from C to C
  • The qwerty row (from q to \) plays the twelve major chords from C to C
  • To play minor chords, hold the shift key
  • To play dominant 7th chords, hold the control key
  • To play minor 7th chords, hold the shift and control keys
  • To play power chords, hold the alt key
